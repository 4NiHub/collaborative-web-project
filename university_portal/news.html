New Research Lab Opening in Computer Science Department

24 января 2026 г.
Admin

We are thrilled to announce the grand opening of the new Artificial Intelligence and Machine Learning Research Laboratory in the Computer Science Department. This state-of-the-art facility represents a significant investment in cutting-edge technology and research capabilities.

The new lab features high-performance computing clusters, advanced GPU arrays for deep learning research, and collaborative workspaces designed to foster innovation. Students and faculty will have access to industry-leading tools and platforms for AI research and development.

"This facility will enable our students to work on real-world AI projects and contribute to groundbreaking research in machine learning, computer vision, and natural language processing," said Dr. Sarah Johnson, Head of the Computer Science Department.

The lab will officially open its doors on February 1st, 2026, with an inaugural symposium featuring presentations from leading AI researchers and industry partners. All students are invited to attend the opening ceremony and tour the new facilities.
